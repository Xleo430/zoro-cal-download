About the App

A living bridge between
faith and daily life

The Zoroastrian Cultural Calendar is a beautifully crafted mobile app designed to keep the ancient Zoroastrian traditions alive and accessible. Whether you follow the Shenshai, Kadmi, or Fasli calendar, the app seamlessly adapts to your tradition and displays the sacred Roj and Mah names alongside today's Gah period.


Track community celebrations, personal milestones, and stay connected to the rhythms of the faith — all in one place, thoughtfully designed for every generation.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🗓️
Three Calendar Systems

Full support for Shenshai, Kadmi, and Fasli calendars. Switch between traditions with a single tap and see the correct Roj and Mah names every day.

🔥
Gah Periods

Stay in tune with the six sacred Gah time periods throughout the day, calculated based on your location's sunrise and sunset times.

🎉
Community Events

Never miss a Jashne, Gahambar, or seasonal celebration. Community events are beautifully highlighted across monthly, daily, and yearly views.

📝
Personal Events

Add birthdays, anniversaries, and personal milestones. Your events live alongside the traditional calendar, bringing the sacred and personal together.

Installation Guide

How to install the
early access build

Android
  1. 1 Tap "Download APK" above. You'll be taken to Google Drive — tap the download icon to save the APK file to your device.
  2. 2 Go to Settings → Security and enable "Install from Unknown Sources" (or "Allow this source" when prompted).
  3. 3 Open the downloaded APK file from your Downloads folder or notification panel.
  4. 4 Tap Install and wait for the installation to complete. Done!
iPhone / iPad
  1. 1 Install TestFlight from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad (it's free, by Apple).
  2. 2 Tap the "Join TestFlight Beta" button above, or open the invite link directly on your device.
  3. 3 You'll be taken to TestFlight — tap "Accept" to join the beta program.
  4. 4 Tap "Install" to download the app. It will appear on your home screen like any other app. Done!
